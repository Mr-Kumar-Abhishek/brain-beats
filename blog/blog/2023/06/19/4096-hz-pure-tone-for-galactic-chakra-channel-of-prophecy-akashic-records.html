  • Location: About a foot and a half above the crown of the head.
  • Function: Channels prophetic insight, allows transcendence of space and time, provides access to the Akashic Records, integrates extremes of polarity (positive/negative), and activates higher shamanic/yogic abilities.
  • Color: Violet‑white, platinum, or iridescent cosmic light.

  • When Balanced: You experience profound intuition, prophetic dreams or visions, a sense of timelessness, access to past‑life and future‑life information, and the ability to consciously navigate non‑ordinary realities.

The galactic chakra is not merely an energy center; it is a portal to the cosmic memory and a receiver of prophetic frequencies.
