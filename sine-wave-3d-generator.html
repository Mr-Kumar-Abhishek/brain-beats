Sine Wave 3D Generator

volume

~ Instructions ~

  1. Input the frequency, along with its co-ordinates namely on x, y and z axis.
  2. Click on 'Add frequency' button to add more frequencies.
  3. Use the red button to remove the frequency.
  4. Play the generator.

Note:

  • It is required to use earphones/headphones.
  • The generator set in such a way that the listener is at the co-ordindate of (x,y,z) = (0,0,0).
  • The maximum and minimum values of frequencies are in hearing range of 20 Hz to 22000 Hz.
  • The maximum and minimum values of 3D co-ordinates are in the range from -1 to 1, typically use fractional values for co-ordinates.
