Binaural Beats

Binaural beats are auditory illusion perceived when two different pure-tone sine waves are presented to a listener dichotically. These are used in modern meditations.

>>
Monaural Beats

Monaural brainwave entrainment is a single pulsing beat, which is also used for meditation. It is created by the convergence of two tones emanating from a single speaker.
